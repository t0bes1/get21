Get 21 Rules

  • The goal of Get21 is to get a hand closer to 21 than the house's hand without going over 21
  • An Ace is worth 11, Face cards (jack, queen, king) are worth 10. All other cards are worth their face value
  • Players initially get 2 cards and can choose "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand)
  • If the player chooses to get another card and goes over 21, they're bust (lose) and don't even get to see the house's hand!
  • If you win a hand you score 3 points. If you lose a hand, you lose 1 point.
  • Get to 10 points to win the game.
